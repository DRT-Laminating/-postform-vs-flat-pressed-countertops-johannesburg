### 🔄 What Are Postform Countertops?

Postform countertops are made by wrapping laminate over the edge of the board.

This creates a smooth, seamless finish.

✔ No visible joins on the front edge  
✔ Better water resistance  
✔ Easy to clean with no dirt-trapping gaps  
✔ Practical for high-use kitchens  

Postform **kitchen tops** are ideal for homes, rentals, and commercial spaces where durability matters most.

---

### 🧱 What Are Flat-Pressed Countertops?

Flat-pressed countertops are manufactured with a flat surface and separate edge finishing.

This creates a more defined and modern look.

✔ Clean, sharp edges  
✔ Ideal for modern kitchen designs  
✔ Works well with custom cabinetry  
✔ Greater flexibility for edge styles and finishes  

Flat-pressed **countertops** are often used in designer kitchens, offices, and retail environments.
